Egg Drop Soup

Image By Sharon Chen

Description

Egg drop soup is a popular dish enjoyed by many. It is commonly found in Chinese restaurants. By incorporating sesame oil into the recipe, you can enhance its flavor. Give it a try and see if you enjoy it as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ¼ teaspoon soy sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives
  • 2 teaspoons water (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch (Optional)
  • 1 drop yellow food coloring (Optional)
  • ½ teaspoon ground white pepper (Optional)
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt (Optional)

Steps

  1. Combine chicken broth, soy sauce, and sesame oil in a small saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil.
  2. Stir water and cornstarch together in a small bowl until cornstarch is dissolved; pour into boiling broth.
  3. Stir in food coloring.
  4. Slowly pour in beaten egg, stirring constantly;
  5. Season with white pepper and salt.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh chives.
